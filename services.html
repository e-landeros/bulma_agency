Brand Development


Our development strategy begins with research into the products and services you offer. We are interested in your business strategy. How you identify to your customers, your unique business proposition and your vision. We develop or acquire internal market research into the forces that shape your industry competition. The barganing power of suppliers and buyers. The threat of substitute products or services and the rivalry among competitors. We then generate customer research; we want to know why your potential customers buy a specific product or service. We use the latest data analytics techniques for data that you may already be collecting about your customers. We aggregate the model with data which includes consumer behavior, disposible income analysis and data gathering techniques like social behavior tracking, and good old fashioned surveys. With this information we proceed to the development of a targeted user/client model which represents the demographic we are trying to reach. All of this information is used in the development of your online presence. Including company logos, web design, layout, colors, and content creation. Specifically blog and social media copywriting will be created to target this model. Continued research and monitoring will allow for expansion to other demographics.
